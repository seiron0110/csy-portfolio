# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

Csy Portfolio

---

# 1. Product Vision

## Purpose

Build a premium personal website that positions Csyronne Galang as a trusted Automation Consultant specializing in AI, CRM, workflow automation, and scalable business systems.

The website should function as a digital consultant—not simply an online resume or portfolio.

Its purpose is to educate visitors, demonstrate expertise through real case studies, build trust, and convert qualified prospects into booked discovery calls.

---

## Problem Statement

Most automation consultants present themselves by listing tools, certifications, or services.

Businesses rarely buy tools.

Businesses buy outcomes.

The portfolio should shift the conversation away from:

- "I know GoHighLevel."
- "I know n8n."
- "I know Make."

and instead communicate:

- I reduce manual work.
- I improve business processes.
- I design scalable systems.
- I help companies grow through automation.

---

## Vision Statement

Create one of the highest-quality automation consultant portfolios on the web.

The website should feel comparable to modern SaaS companies such as Vercel, Stripe, Linear, and OpenAI.

Visitors should immediately feel:

- This consultant understands business.
- This consultant explains technical concepts clearly.
- This consultant builds reliable systems.
- This is someone I want to work with.

---

## Success Definition

The website succeeds when visitors:

- Understand the value proposition within five seconds.
- View at least one case study.
- Develop trust before reaching the About page.
- Book a discovery call.

The website should become a long-term business asset that grows alongside Csyronne's consulting career.
